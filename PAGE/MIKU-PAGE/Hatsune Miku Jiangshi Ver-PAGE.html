Hatsune Miku Jiangshi Ver

"Honda Roaro's original illustration [Hatsune Miku Jiangshi Ver.] has been made into a 1/7 scale figure !"

The original illustration by popular illustrator Honda Roaro, [Hatsune Miku Jiangshi Ver.], is now available as a 1/7 scale figure!
With her hands hanging down in a resentful manner and her legs stretched out straight, she expresses the unique pose of a Jiangshi, while also exuding a certain adorableness.
The dissatisfied expression with the left cheek puffed out is exquisitely cute and sure to tickle the hearts of anyone who sees it.

The translucent white skin texture, the delicate luster of the rosary, and the intricate gradation of the hair color create a stunning finish.
Also, be sure to take a look at the open-back outfit, which wasn't visible in the illustration.
The base is decorated with cracked cobblestones and a black fence, creating a dark and fantastical world view.
Be sure to get your hands on the mysterious yet somehow lovely Jiangshi version of Hatsune Miku.
