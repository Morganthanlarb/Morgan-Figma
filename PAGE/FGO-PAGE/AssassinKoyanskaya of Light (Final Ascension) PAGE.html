Assassin/Koyanskaya of Light (Final Ascension)

"Koyanskaya of Light is here! Weaklings, prepare yourselves."

From "Fate/Grand Order" comes a 1/4 scale figure of the Assassin-class servant Koyanskaya of Light!

Koyanskaya's distinctive design, including her curled twintails, fluffy tail and glasses, has been faithfully captured in figure form. Her bunny suit-style costume features real net tights, a specialty of the B-style series. Various other elements of her Final Ascension illustration have also been created in figure form, such as her gigantic rifle, antique chair and playing card. Be sure to add the charming Koyanskaya to your collection!
