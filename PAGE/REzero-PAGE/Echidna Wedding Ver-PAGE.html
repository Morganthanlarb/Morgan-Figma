Echidna: Wedding Ver.

"I can no longer satisfy my curiosity without you."

From the popular anime series "Re:ZERO -Starting Life in Another World-" comes a 1/7 scale figure of Echidna. the Witch of Greed.
Echidna has been brought into figure form in a wedding version to match the previously released Rem: Wedding Ver., Ram: Wedding Ver. and Emilia: Wedding Ver. figures.
Opposite of the previous figures in the series, Echidina's outfit features a black dress and veil. The figure also features translucent, long white hair and an expressive smile, perfectly capturing Echidna's charm.
