Figma Guts: Berserk Armour ver.

Included with the first limited edition Berserk manga!

A collaboration between the popular figma action figure series and the latest volume of the Berserk manga brings a figma of Guts wearing his berserker armour!

  • The high quality action figure is based on the original character designs and fully supervised by Kentaro Miura himself.
  • The figure is sculpted by Max Factory's Masaki Asai, who has faithfully reproduced the armor's design while still keeping the figure posable.
  • There are two different head parts included, a 'Beast of Darkness' head part as well as a sane head part.
  • A luminous Schierke is also included to recreate Guts and Schierke's unison scene.
  • The figure is 160mm in height and features 14 different points of articulation for all sorts of different poses.
  • Two postcards illustrated by Kentaro Miura are also included.
